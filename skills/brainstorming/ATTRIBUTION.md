# Attributions and Citations

Substantially redesigned from [obra/superpowers:brainstorming](https://github.com/obra/superpowers/tree/main/skills/brainstorming) by Jesse Vincent.

## Research informing the design

### Process / dialogue

- de Bono, _Six Thinking Hats_ — phase-appropriate modes; Blue-hat synthesizer role
- UK Design Council's Double Diamond framework (referenced as a starting point — divergent/convergent distinction matters; named multi-stage phases lack outcome evidence)
- Rapoport's Rules for steelman critique (restate, agree, note what you learned, then rebut)
- Steelman Solitaire (LessWrong) — sequential steelman/counter moves in 1:1 contexts

### Mixed-initiative / agent pacing

- IUI 2025 MIND workshop — bridging agent-driven automation and direct manipulation ([dl.acm.org/10.1145/3708557.3716160](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3708557.3716160))
- NN/g on confirmation dialogs — gates belong on irreversible actions, undo preferred for normal flow ([nngroup.com/articles/confirmation-dialog](https://www.nngroup.com/articles/confirmation-dialog))
- Inner Thoughts — proactive agent motivations rather than reactive turn-taking ([arxiv:2501.00383](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.00383))
- "Mandatory Thinking Can Backfire" ([arxiv:2602.07796](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.07796))

### Multi-agent dispatch / persona

- Cambridge Design Science 2025 — parallel >> sequential >> collective for multi-persona prompting ([cambridge.org](https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/design-science/article/enhancing-design-concept-diversity-multipersona-prompting-strategies-for-large-language-models/3B346E253508337A4EE899499BE49D9B))
- Persona prompting double-edged sword ([arxiv:2408.08631](https://arxiv.org/abs/2408.08631))
- Choosing Your Experts ([arxiv:2509.20553](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.20553))
- 2024–2026 MAD literature: skepticism toward naive debate ([arxiv:2511.07784](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.07784), [arxiv:2509.23055](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.23055))
- Cross-context review outperforms in-context ([arxiv:2603.12123](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.12123))

### Creative homogeneity / diversity

- Creative homogeneity across LLMs ([arxiv:2501.19361](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.19361))
- Post-RLHF probability sharpening as root cause ([arxiv:2502.20408](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.20408))
- Verbalized Sampling — 1.6–2.1× diversity, training-free ([arxiv:2510.01171](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.01171))
- Diversity-aware retrieval / MMR ([arxiv:2502.09017](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.09017))
- DPP-based selection ([arxiv:2512.12072](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.12072))
- HAICo / CreativeDC — distinct rosters for divergent vs convergent passes ([arxiv:2512.18388](https://arxiv.org/html/2512.18388v2), [arxiv:2512.23601](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.23601))
- FlexMind — premature critique suppresses idea remoteness ([arxiv:2509.21685](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.21685))
